Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)

  • Advanced technique allowing up to 5,000 graft transplants
  • No stitches or sutures required
  • Leaves no linear scar, ensuring a natural look
  • Facilitates rapid recovery with minimal discomfort
  • Ideal for patients seeking a less invasive option

Follicular Unit Transplants (FUT)

  • Perfect for large-scale procedures
  • Optimal for clients with limited donor areas
  • Can effectively incorporate and camouflage old scars
  • Maximizes graft yield for extensive hair restoration

Scalp Micropigmentation (SMP)

  • Non-surgical solution to camouflage hair loss
  • Utilizes medical-grade pigments for a natural appearance
  • Completed in just 1 or 2 sessions
  • Ideal for those seeking a low-maintenance solution

Beard, Mustache & Eyebrow Hair Transplants

  • Led by a Board-Certified Facial Plastic Surgeon
  • Offers FUE, FUT, and innovative Hybrid procedures
